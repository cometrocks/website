TLDR

To keep things simple, here is a non binding summary of the Terms of Service, highlighting the key commercial terms of the binding terms of service agreement.

Overview
  • Comet provides an ecommerce platform for merchants to create online satellite stores. Free Trial
Free Trial
  • Qualified merchants can test Comet's platform for free. Each merchant receives a 3 month free trial period where merchants can transact up to 100.000 EUR via Comet's platform without any charges besides the Payment Service Provider Reimbursement fee described below.
Fees
  • Transaction Fee: Merchants pay a 5% transaction fee on sales exceeding 100.000 EUR via Comet's platform.
  • Platform Fee: The Platform Fee is waived for all standard merchants. Additional platform services can be requested.
  • Reimbursement Fee: A reimbursement fee covers payment processing costs, but only applies if Comet's checkout is used. This fee is set by Stripe, Comet's current payment service.
  • Professional services: Any additional professional services requested by the client will be agreed in advance via a separate order form.
Services
  • Comet provides platform access, maintenance, support services, and payment processing integrations.
  • Comet assists with onboarding merchants onto the platform.
Term & Termination
  • Initial term is 12 months, auto-renewing every 3 months.
  • Either party can terminate without cause with 30 days notice.
  • Comet can immediately terminate for non-payment or violations.
Key merchant responsibilities:
  • Complying with platform policies and legal requirements
  • Securing their own connected systems
  • Paying fees

In summary, Comet offers a 3 month free trial before transaction fees apply. Reimbursement fees only apply if Comet's checkout is used. Key terms cover fees, termination, services provision, and merchant compliance obligations
